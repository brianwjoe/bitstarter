Tired of paying too much to your ISP? Bytepushers is an easy to use, no frills ISP that gives you what you want - Bytes when you want them. Most ISPs today make their money through oversubscription - by charging their customers for more data than they actually use each month. The normal consumer ends up paying more for their data than they should, padding the pockets of their ISP and subsidizing the bandwidth hogs on the system. Bytepushers gives customers the data they want and charges them only for what they use.

Would you hire your plumber to build you a website? That is what we believe is the biggest problem with your ISP. They don't have the expertise of the incentive to build the services that you really want, and instead make you choose from a platter of bad options. The heart of our solution is a frame and packet tracking and billing system of every ethernet frame and IP packet on any service provider's network. Our philosophy is based on the following principles:

  • Virtual Networking - Thanks to the invention of the virtual switch, commodity webservers are now able to access and perform operations on a passing packet with minimal overhead.
  • Web Scale Tools - By using proven open source tools Pioneered by web giants such as Google and Facebook, we are able to scale horizontally to keep track of the massive amounts of flow data from traditional networks.
  • Public Cloud Infrastructure - by leveraging Public Cloud infrastructure, we are able to scale with our customers without requiring new hardware deployments.

    What is Bytepusher?
    Bytepusher is an Internet Service Provider that only sells one thing - bytes. This is what makes Bytepushers different from other ISPs. Other ISPs charge customers for more data than they actually use and to make matters worse, they add on extra services that nobody needs. Bytepushers charges you only for the data that you use and we've optimized our entire business around it. Get connected in 24 hours with no monthly commitments, no hassles, and no overages.
    How does Bytepusher work?
    Bytepusher's engineers have designed an integrated 3G/4G/Cable/DSL router that automatically switches between the best broadband connection to the internet. This hotspot can choose between different broadand providers including 3G, 4G, DSL, Cable, and Fiber networks. Customers can choose and configure their their preferred broadband method based on their schedule, budget, and preferred carrier through our simple web portal, which gives customers an instant view of all times of their data usage and monthly payments.
